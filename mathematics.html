Mathematics: The Language of the Universe

Mathematics is the study of topics such as quantity, structure, space, and change. It provides the tools and language for science, engineering, and finance. Explore the foundational branches below.

-- Arithmetic --

The fundamental branch of mathematics, concerning the properties of the traditional operations on numbers: addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

Explore Arithmetic

-- Algebra --

The study of mathematical symbols and the rules for manipulating those symbols. It is the unifying thread of almost all of mathematics.

Explore Algebra

-- Geometry --

The study of shapes, sizes, positions of figures, and the properties of space. It is one of the oldest mathematical sciences.

Explore Geometry

-- Calculus --

The mathematical study of continuous change. Calculus is the language used to describe the motion of planets, the flow of fluids, and the rates of chemical reactions.

Explore Calculus
