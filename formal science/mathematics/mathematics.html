Mathematics

Mathematics is the study of topics such as quantity (numbers), structure, space, and change. It has no generally accepted definition. Mathematicians seek out patterns and use them to formulate new conjectures.

Algebra

The study of mathematical symbols and the rules for manipulating them.

Geometry

Concerned with properties of space such as distance, shape, size, and position.

Calculus

The mathematical study of continuous change, a major branch of modern math.
