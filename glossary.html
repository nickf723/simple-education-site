Terms

Function

A relation that assigns each input exactly one output; often written as $f(x)$.

Mathematics

Variable

A symbol representing an unknown or changeable quantity within an expression or equation.

AlgebraMathematics

Factor

To rewrite an expression as a product of simpler expressions; e.g., $x^2-9=(x-3)(x+3)$.

Algebra

Angle

Formed by two rays with a common endpoint; measured in degrees or radians.

Geometry

Unit Circle

The circle of radius 1 centered at the origin; defines $\sin\theta$ and $\cos\theta$ as coordinates.

Trigonometry

Derivative

Instantaneous rate of change; the slope of the tangent line to a function at a point.

Calculus

Integral

Accumulation or signed area under a curve; inverse operation to differentiation (FTC links them).

Calculus

Mean

Arithmetic average; sum of values divided by count.

Statistics

Standard Deviation

A measure of spread around the mean; square root of variance.

Statistics

Probability

A number between 0 and 1 describing the likelihood of an event.

Statistics

Vector

An element of a vector space; can be represented as an ordered list of components.

Linear Algebra

Matrix

A rectangular array of numbers representing a linear transformation or system of equations.

Linear Algebra

Algorithm

A finite set of step-by-step instructions for solving a class of problems.

Computer Science

Proof

A logically sound argument establishing the truth of a statement from accepted premises.
